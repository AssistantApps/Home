Companion of the Forest

Do you play The Forest? Tired of searching for item locations while playing? Companion of the Forest is an interactive Forest Map that shows you where items in the game are. Whether you're a new or returning player, this app's got you covered with locations of items from the original Forest and it's sequel, Sons of the Forest!
